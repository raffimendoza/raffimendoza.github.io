Projects

Sentiment Analysis

What does it mean to call a woman a “Karen”? The origins of any meme are hard to pin down, and this one has spread with the same intensity as the coronavirus, and often in parallel with it. Karens are “the police women of all human behavior.” Karens don’t believe in vaccines. Karens have short hair. Karens are selfish. Confusingly, Karens are both the kind of petty enforcers who patrol other people’s failures at social distancing, and the kind of entitled women who refuse to wear a mask because it’s a “muzzle.” They are likely to be a woman at the age of 40 or over and hold microaggressions against racial groups.

Proposed security & attendance system to keep track of students and staff accessing labratory rooms. Embedded system consists of a Raspberry Pi Zero W, an RFID-RC522 Module, multiple RFID tags, 16x2 LCD screen. and a red/green LED.

Pet Renter

Due to isolation caused from the pandemic, people unprecedentedly need companionship. However, not everyone could afford to raise a pet or be patient enough to take care of them, some of them can not provide an appropriate environment for long term adoption. In this case, renting is a good choice for those lonely people, while there are plenty of pets abandoned because of their previous masters’ impulsion and the adoption center can hardly afford them. We plan to provide a web app which could subdue the situation by allowing customers to temporarily take care of those cute animals before they rent them, and leave those adoption centers another way to raise funds.
